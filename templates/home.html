WELCOME TO THE E-VOTING SYSTEM


Easy to Use

E-Voting is very user friendly and voting can be done anytime and from anywhere within seconds.



Cost Effective

The complete election can be conducted for as low as Rs 10,000. Cost includes the cost of setting up the voting process and live result counting. No need to spend a money on election booths, manpower, EVM machines, ballot paper, security, counting etc.


Key electronic voting features.

Stay protected against double voting and other forms of vote manipulation. Get results instantly and dive deeper into voter analytics.


A reliable online voting tool.

Run online elections for an important event or manage consistent, recurring votes.


Candidate Profiles

Helps voters choose by including a biography, vision, campaign promises and contact information. By Uploading a candidate photo and their information


Anonymous Voting

All voting choices are confidential and cannot be linked to the voter for elections. And results can be hidden until the election ends for further anonymity.
